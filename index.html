Donald Pearson

Graphic Designer & Software Engineer

Here to bring your vision to life

Donald is a graphic designer who hopes to aid people in bringing their vision to life. He graduated from Purdue University with a Bachelor's Degree in Graphic Design. Combined with his degree, Donald has over 14 years of professional experience of creating, designing, and working with clients under his belt. Some tools in his repertoire includes Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, and Indesign. Donald offers logo design and logo vectorization, among other graphic design work that consist of brochure design, label design, and banner design to just name a few.
